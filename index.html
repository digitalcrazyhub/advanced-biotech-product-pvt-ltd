Advanced Regenerative Mesh for Surgical Excellence

Surgicoll-Mesh® is a implantable Biocompatible Type-1 Collagen mesh that is flexible, strong yet soft. The Collagen membrane is also supple when hydrated and handles like natural tissue. It readily conforms to the surgical site upon application and is easily sutured. Surgicoll-Mesh® is a reconstituted Type-1 Collagen mesh free of contaminants like lipids, elastin and other immunogenic proteins. The Collagen is used in the Surgicoll-Mesh® product is based on a patented technology that ensures the high purity of Type-1 Collagen. It is processed under GMP conditions with stringent USP quality tests as per WHO (World Health Organization) and other international standards.

Surgicoll-Mesh® is a Biocompatible and sterilized Type-1 Collagen matrix for Cartilage Regeneration, Soft Tissue & Muscle Flap Reinforcement, Dural Repair, Intra Oral Surgical Applica-tions including Oral Cancer and Pre-Cancerous Lesions, Colo-Rectal, Urethral & Vaginal Prolapse, Pelvic Floor Reconstruction, Urethral Sling & Third Degree Burn to Accommodate the Skin Graft Needs.
