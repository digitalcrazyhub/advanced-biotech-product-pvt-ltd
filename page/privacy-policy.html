Third-party disclosure

We use Google Analytics for visitor trend analysis. We may share your information with third parties only:

Data retention & deletion

We retain personal information as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes it was collected for, including satisfying any legal, regulatory, accounting, or reporting requirements.

UK / EEA / Switzerland residents
