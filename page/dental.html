What is Surgicoll-Mesh®

An Innovative Scaffold That Becomes You

Surgicoll-Mesh® is a sterile, implantable Collagen scaffold that acts as a framework your body progressively integrates, remodels, and replaces with functional host tissue leaving no foreign material behind.

97% Pure Type-I
Collagen

Unlike conventional Collagen membranes made by lyophilization with random fiber arrangement and high porosity (>800 µm), Surgicoll-Mesh® is prepared by a proprietary sediment process yielding native parallel fibers with just 20 µm average porosity maximizing cell infiltration and regenerative factor penetration.

Its bioactivity is enhanced through controlled phosphorylation of selected amino acids, which accelerates cell migration to the wound bed and triggers neo-vascularization within 4–5 days of application.

  • Chronic ulcers and Diabetic ulcer treatment.
  • Third-Degree Burn to accommodate the Skin Graft needs.
  • Orthopedic Surgery - Osteochondral resurfacing, synovial tissue implant, repair of rotator cuff, patellar, Achilles, biceps, quadriceps and other tendons.
