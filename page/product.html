About

Surgicoll-Mesh®

Implantable, Bioresorbable Tissue Regenerative Sterile Type-1 Collagen Matrix

Surgicoll-Mesh® is a biocompatible Collagen mesh that is flexible, strong yet soft Collagen membrane that is supple when hydrated and handles like natural tissue; it readily conforms to the surgical site upon application and is easily sutured.

Surgicoll-Mesh® is a reconstituted Type-1 Collagen mesh free of contaminants like lipids, elastin and other immunogenic proteins. The Collagen used in Surgicoll-Mesh® is based on patented technology that ensures high purity Type-1 Collagen.

Surgicoll-Mesh® is Biocompatible and sterilized Type-1 Collagen matrix for soft tissue & muscle flap reinforcement, dural repair, colorectal, urethral & vaginal prolapse, pelvic floor reconstruction and orthopaedic procedures.

Why Surgicoll-Mesh®

Clinically Effective Through Dual Action Technology

Surgicoll-Mesh® delivers unmatched clinical performance by combining biocompatibility and bioactivity, a combination no other biomatrix can replicate.

Biocompatible
High-Purity Type-1 Collagen

Made of native, un-crosslinked pure Type-1 Collagen (>97% pure), completely free of immunogenic proteins, lipids, elastin, Type-1II Collagen, and glycosaminoglycans. Parallel, organized fibers mimic native skin structure with ~20µm porosity ideal for cell infiltration and tissue regeneration.

Bioactive
Phosphorylated for Cell Signalling

The Collagen is phosphorylated, creating unique cell signal transduction capabilities. This attracts undifferentiated stem cells and converts them to specific cell lines for tissue repair. Improved cell signalling produces the molecules required for repair and regeneration a feature unique to Surgicoll-Mesh®.

Neo-vascularization Within 4–5 Days

Collagen phosphorylation attracts cells, regenerates tissue, and stimulates blood capillaries and granulation within just 4 to 5 days after application. This clinically proven rapid neo-vascularization is not shown with any other biomatrix product.

Ref: Acta Medica International, 3(1), 146 (2016)

Ultra-Pure Extraction Process
Patented papain-based purification with reducing and delipidation agents yields >97% pure Type-I Collagen. The stringent process eliminates all immunogenic contaminants.
Native Parallel Fibril Architecture
Sedimentation process creates organized parallel fibers with ~20µm porosity matching native skin structure. Competing lyophilized products have random, non-native fibrillar structures with >800µm porosity.
Phosphorylation — Enhanced Bioactivity
Charge modification through phosphorylation leads to cell signal transduction, attracting neuronal cells (documented at Stanford University, CA, USA). This exceptional bioactivity is exclusive to Surgicoll-Mesh®.
Ethylene Oxide Sterilization
ETO gas sterilization does not denature the protein, unlike gamma sterilization used by most other products. Each unit is individually sterilized in sterile double packaging.
